Who Are Sydney Agudong Parents: Kenneth And Karen Agudong?

March 2024 · 5 minute read

Sydney Agudong who is best known for recurring roles in TV Land’s Teachers and Nickelodeon’s Nicky, Ricky, Dicky & Dawn has signed on to play Lilo’s older sister, Nani, in the Disney’s Lilo & Stitch live-action remake. Following the casting news, fans for a while debated about colorism in Disney movies, referring to Sydney’s lighter skin tone as compared to Nani’s animated character. Sydney, if you don’t already know, is a British-Irish with Hawaiian and Filipino lineage. And so in the rest of the writing, we shall tell you about her parents Kenneth And Karen Agudong.

Who Are Sydney Agudong Parents?

Sydney Agudong was born in Kauai, Hawaii, and later also raised here, by Karen Agudong and Kenneth Agudong. She came to them on 19 August 2004 and therefore turned 18 years old in 2022.

Keep reading to find out more about Sydney’s parents.

Meet Kenneth Agudong, Sydney Agudong Dad

Kenneth Agudong is the son of Luciano Balisacan Agudong and Solina Asuncion. Solina, his mother, was born in Ilocos Norte, Philippines.

  • Kenneth Agudong Age

Kenneth John Agudong was born in April 1960. So, he turned 63 years old in 2023.

  • What Is Kenneth Agudong Ethnicity?

Kenneth Agudong’s ethnicity is a mix of Filipino and Polynesian.

  • Kenneth Agudong Job

Kenneth Agudong is a realtor at Aloha Island Properties in Lihue, Hawaii. This is the same company where his wife also began her Real Estate career in 1997. She is also the former owner/ broker of Aloha Island Properties. She joined the real estate firm in 1997 and purchased it in 2005. After owning the brokerage for over 10 years, Karen decided to downsize and focus primarily on assisting clients again with sales for residential, vacant land, and condominium properties.

  • Is Kenneth Agudong On Instagram?

Yes. Kenneth Agudong could be found on Instagram @kaylijon where he entertained 7 posts and 173 followers as of 16 April 2023.

Other than that, he also showed now and then glimpses of his life on ‘Kenneth Agudong’ Facebook.

Meet Karen Agudong, Sydney Agudong Mom

Karen, with the full name Karen Ligon Agudong, is the daughter of Douglas Wister Ligon (the son of Wister Henry Ligon and Gertrude L. “Trudy” Lundergan) and Ellen Elizabeth Finks (the daughter of Robert Mark Finks and Josephine Elizabeth “Betty” King).

  • Karen Agudong Age

Karen Agudong was born in February 1971. So, she turned 52 years old in 2023.

  • What Is Karen Agudong Ethnicity?

Karen Agudong’s ethnicity is a blend of English, German, Irish, and Scottish.

On the paternal side, her grandfather was of English, and some Scottish, descent and her grandmother was Irish and German.

On the maternal side though, her grandfather had German, English, Scottish, and Irish ancestry, and her grandmother came from a German bloodline.

  • Karen Agudong Job

Per LinkedIn, Karen Agudong has been a full-time realtor for over 26 years on Kauai. She describes that she specializes in residential, condominium, and vacant land sales island-wide.

Also, it’s been cited that since February 2018 until now, Karen has been a realtor with eXp Realty in Kauai, Hawaii.

Karen back in the day studied Bachelor of Arts with a Psychology major at the University of Hawaii at Manoa from 1995 – 1998. After this, she worked in the Hospitality Industry at the beautiful Kaua’i Marriott Resort for a while. And it is while working there that her drive and enthusiasm for sales and marketing inspired her to pursue real estate instead.

  • Is Karen Agudong On Instagram?

Yes. Karen Agudong can be found on Instagram. As of 15 April 2023, his account @karen_agudong_365 included 92 posts and 1,611 followers.

She also showed now and then glimpses of her life on ‘Karen Ligon Agudong’ Facebook.

Besides, he and Kenneth also appear occasionally on their daughter Sienna’s IG @sienaagudong.

Related FAQs

  • Are Sydney Agudong Parents Still Married?

Indeed. Sydney Agudong’s parents were still married and together as of the time of this writing. Sydney’s dad even cited this, i.e., the status of his marriage, on his social media profile.

  • How Many Kids Do Sydney Agudong Parents Have?

Sydney Agudong is not the only child of her parents. She is one of four daughters.

Karen’s realtor/broker BIO on the internet also presents that she is blessed to have a wonderful, supportive husband, and four daughters, 22 yr. old, Sydney, 18 yr. old, Siena, and her older daughters, Kayli and Sarah. And that, between a successful real estate career, she loves her wonderful family.

Like Sydney, Siena also is an up-and-coming actress. She has already seen decent fame with the Nickelodeon series Star Falls (2018), the film Alex & Me (2018), the Netflix series No Good Nick (2019), the Disney Channel Original Movie Upside-Down Magic (2020), and Netflix’s recent Resident Evil (2022). Besides, Siena also has earned several “Young Entertainer” and “Young Artist” award nominations. In 2017, she even won “Best Guest Starring Young Actress 12 & Under” at the Young Entertainers Awards held at LA’s Universal Studios.

  • Where Do Sydney Agudong Parents Reside?

Sydney Agudong’s parents had been residing in their home in Lihue, Hawaii as of April 2023.

Also, on her Facebook, Sydney’s mom mentioned coming from Kapaa, Hawaii. Her job profile on the internet also tells us that the matriarch has lived in Kauai, Hawaii for the past 30 years. It goes on to spill that she was raised in middle Tennessee but moved to Hawaii to finish up college and stayed.
