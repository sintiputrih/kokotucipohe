Jace Norman

October 2024 · 4 minute read

Jace Norman is an American actor who is best known for his role as Henry Hart in the Nickelodeon television series, Henry Danger. He commenced acting in 2012.  

Jace’s Bio & Family

The 20-years-of-age actor was born with the named Jace Lee Norman on March 21, 2000, in Corrales, New Mexico, the United States. He is among the three children of his parents, Scott Norman and Ileane Lee Norman. Jace’s mother, Ileane is a realtor. Besides, his family consists of Jace’s two older siblings: Xander Norman and Glory Norman.

Career & Net Worth

For his outstanding acting stints, the young actor amasses a commanding net worth of $3 million. The major part of this sum is derived from his television work as Henry Hart/Kid Danger in the comedy series, Henry Danger. He appeared in the lead role from 2014 to 2020.

Jace initiated his acting career as Finch in the American comedy series, Jessie, in 2012. He then went on to appear in several television movies and series like The Thundermans (2013), Splitting Adam (2015), Rufus (2016), and The Adventures of Kid Danger (2018). Furthermore, his recent role was in the comedy series, Game Shakers, from 2018 to 2019, as Henry Hart.
