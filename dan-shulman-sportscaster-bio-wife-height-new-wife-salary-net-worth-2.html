Dan Shulman Biography

Dan Shulman is a Canadian sportscaster who was born and brought up in Toronto, Ontario. Currently, he calls select Toronto Blue Jays telecasts on Sportsnet and hosts the baseball-themed podcast, Swing and a Belt with Dan Shulman. Additionally, he serves as a play-by-play announcer for ESPN men’s college basketball,  Major League Baseball games, and post-season MLB coverage on ESPN Radio.

Dan Shulman Blue Jays

Dan joined TSN in 1995 where he served as a play-by-play voice broadcast of Toronto Blue Jays baseball games. He served as a commentator alongside former Blue Jays catcher Buck Martinez. Additionally, during his time at TSN, he served as a secondary play-by-play voice for NHL hockey and lead announcer for NBA basketball. He also reported on CHL games and served as a cohost on TSN Inside Sports and the World Series alongside Dave Hodge.

Shulman served at TSN for seven years before leaving then returning in 2011 as an analyst and contributor.
