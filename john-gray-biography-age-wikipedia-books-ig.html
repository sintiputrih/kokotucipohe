John Gray Biography, Age, Wikipedia, Books, IG

Pastor John Gray is an American personality, pastor, gospel singer and TV presenter. He is an associate pastor at Lakewood church and has been holding the position for many years. He was born on June 26, 1973, in Cincinnati, Ohio, United States.

He has worked as the host of the John Gray World television program and also appeared on the show, The Preachers on the Fox Channel. He has also worked as an associate pastor at Osteen’s Lakewood Church in Houston earlier to go to Greenville.

Pastor John is being treated in critical care as he is suffering from Saddle Pulmonary Embolism in the pulmonary artery and more lung blood clots.
