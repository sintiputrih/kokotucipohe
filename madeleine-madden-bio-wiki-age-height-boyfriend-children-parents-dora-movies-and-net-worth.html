Madeleine Madden Biography

Before her international fame, at the age of 13, Madden had already won the hearts of her countrymen when she became the first teenager in Australia to deliver an address to the nation when on the future of Indigenous Australians.

Madeleine Madden Actress

Madden made her debut in the tv world at age 8. She has had a number of starring roles in Australia’s first indigenous teen drama, Ready for This, and in the critically acclaimed Redfern Now. Madeleine has also starred in The Moodys, Jack Irish, My Place, and The Code. In 2016 she starred in the miniseries Tomorrow When the War Began which is based on the John Marsden series of young adult books.

In 2018 she portrayed Marion Quade in the miniseries Picnic at Hanging Rock, Crystal Swan in the TV miniseries Mystery Road and Immy DuPain in the series Pine Gap. She has been cast as Egwene al Vere in Amazon’s upcoming adaptation of The Wheel of Time novels

Madden has also starred in several films such as Around the Block.  Madden made her big Hollywood debut at age 21 when she was cast as Sammy in the 2019 Nickelodeon film Dora and the Lost City of Gold.
