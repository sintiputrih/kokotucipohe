Get To Know Actor Tobie Donovan Bio, Height, Partner, Age, Parents

May 2024 · 5 minute read

Heartstopper is a British coming-of-age romance based on 2016’s webcomic and graphic novel of the same name by Alice Oseman, which ended up selling over a million print copies.

And on the series that dropped on Netflix on 22 April 2022, actor Tobie Donovan’s Isaac Henderson is the only role specifically created for the Netflix series as the character does not feature in Alice Oseman’s original graphic novel. That was one more reason this turned out to be Tobie’s biggest dream come true.

Now, read through this ‘Tobie Donovan Bio’ and find out all about his height, partner, age, parents, and Heartstopper journey.

Tobie Donovan On Heartstopper

For Tobie Donovan, Hearstopper has truly been the craziest and most exciting experience of his life. He stars as one new addition Isaac Henderson, alongside Rhea Norwood as Imogen Heaney.

Isaac is an introverted member of Charlie (actor Joe Locke), Tao (actor William Gao), and Elle’s (actress Yasmin Finney) friend circle. He would rather be reading a novel most of his time than be dealing with any drama. But this does not mean that he is not a solid, supportive friend in the core group.

Isaac may not have been born by creator Alice Oseman. But he sure gets her approval as well. Most recently on Twitter, she could not stop talking about him. “He’s a bookworm and doesn’t say much, but when he does, it’s usually very insightful and/or funny. He doesn’t have a huge arc of his own this season, but I have maaaany plans for him in future seasons”, about the onscreen newcomer. She wished him better luck with the actor that portrayed her favorite character in the show.

Most recently on his Instagram, Tobie also thanked the series’ executive producers Patrick Walters, Euros Lyn, and casting director Daniel Edwards-Guy, along with Alice Oseman, for believing in him and trusting him to tell Isaac’s story.

Like the original story, Heartstopper, the live-action version of Heartstopper follows two schoolboys, and the romantic tensions simmer beneath their developing friendship. Gentle Charlie and rugby-loving Nick meet at secondary school and quickly discover their unlikely friendship blossoming into an unexpected romance. All along the couple with their circle of friends, including Isaac, navigate the ever-relatable journey of self-discovery and acceptance, supporting each other as they learn to find their most authentic selves.

Its creator Alice, at first, did not make any money out of it. It was just a passion project for her. She was just uploading it for free online. And now it has been made into a TV show, she feels really weird about it.
