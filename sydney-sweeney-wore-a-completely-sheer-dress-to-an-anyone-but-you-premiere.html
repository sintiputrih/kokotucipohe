Sydney Sweeney wore a completely sheer dress to an Anyone But You premiere

June 2024 · 3 minute read

I genuinely think Sydney Sweeney is so pretty and sexy, but I have nothing but sympathy for her as I watch her try to navigate how to dress her figure with her very large bust. I kind of think Sydney doesn’t have a great style eye, and maybe she depends too much on a stylist who doesn’t know exactly what they’re doing? All I’m saying is that her style can be really hit or miss.

These photos are from the Australian premiere of Anyone But You, which was actually filmed entirely IN Australia. Glen Powell didn’t bother flying out again, so Sydney got the most attention on the carpet. Of course she wore little more than a beach cover up! If this dress was knee-length, I would be convinced that it IS a beach coverup, something to wear over your bikini when you go to the beach bar. While that’s sort of on-brand for Australia, for a big premiere it’s… not the best look. InStyle had a nicer write-up about it, basically claiming that Sydney is on-trend with “naked dressing,” visible bras and panties, lots of sheer. That… was a trend this year?

Meanwhile, it looks like Sydney is definitely still with her fiance Jonathan Davino. He’s been around, on the periphery of Sydney’s promotional tour, although he has not walked any of the carpets with her. He flew to Australia too – the Mail got photos of him. Back in April/May, there was a lot of talk about Sydney possibly dumping him for Glen Powell. Guess that didn’t happen (although I still think something did happen around that time).
