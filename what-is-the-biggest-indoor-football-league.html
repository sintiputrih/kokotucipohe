What is the biggest Indoor Football League?

August 2024 · 2 minute read

The Indoor Football League (IFL) stands out as the biggest indoor football league, recognized for its rich history, prominence, and high-caliber athletes. Here's a comprehensive look at what makes the IFL the leading force in professional indoor football:

League Overview

  • The IFL was established in 2008 through the merger of the Intense Football League and United Indoor Football, making it one of the oldest active professional indoor football leagues in North America.
  • With 16 teams in two conferences, the IFL has one of the largest numbers of currently active teams among indoor football leagues.
  • The league plays a 16-game schedule, providing fans with an extensive season of thrilling indoor football action.

Player Compensation

  • IFL players earn between US$250–500 per game played, with additional bonuses for players on the winning team each week.
  • Some teams also offer housing for their players during the season, demonstrating a commitment to their players' well-being[5].

Professionalism and Talent

  • The IFL features the world's best indoor football players and coaches, positioning itself as a league of choice for top-tier talent.
  • The league's high-paying nature and exposure have led to more than 150 players from the IFL signing contracts with NFL or CFL teams, a testament to the caliber of athletes it attracts.

Expansion and Footprint

  • The IFL is the only indoor football league with a national footprint, boasting 16 teams, with further expansion expected in the coming years.
  • Notably, 10 of the 16 teams are based in top 50 media markets, indicating the league's significant presence in key locations.

Unique Product and Leadership

  • The IFL offers a unique product, with games played on a 50-yard field surrounded by padded dasher boards, distinctive from traditional outdoor football.
  • Led by Commissioner Todd Tryon, the IFL is helmed by an individual with a deep understanding of the league, having served as a player, coach, and team owner.

Mission, Vision, and Core Values

  • The IFL's mission is centered around serving fans, sponsors, and markets with the highest level of professional indoor football, coupled with integrity, affordability, and passion, on and off the field.
  • It aspires to be the No. 1 source of family-friendly sports entertainment through professionalism, sportsmanship, quality, and stability.
  • The league's core values emphasize respect, integrity, leadership, and quality, reflecting its commitment to professionalism and positive impact in the communities it serves.

the Indoor Football League (IFL) has solidified its position as the biggest and most prominent indoor football league in the United States, offering a compelling blend of high-quality competition, player welfare, and a unique brand of family-friendly sports entertainment.
