Jacky Lai is an actress best known for Shadowhunters (2016) and V-Wars (2019). She was born in Toronto, Canada.

Jacky Lai is starring as Kaylee Vo in ‘V-Wars’ (2019)

V-Wars is a Netflix series adapted from the IDW graphic novel by Jonathan Maberry. The story revolves around a mysterious virus transforming humans into vampires setting up a war between the two species.

The series stars Ian Somerhalder as Dr. Luther Swann, Kyle Breitkopf as Dez, Laura Vandervoort as Mila Dubov, Adrian Holmes as Michael Fayne, Peter Outerbridge as Calix Niklos, and Kimberly-Sue Murray as Danika.

A brief look at Jacky Lai’s Career/Wiki/Bio - What is her Net Worth?

Jacky Lai is a model turned actress featuring in several movies and TV series, all of which has boosted her net worth.
Source: Listal

Lai began her career as a model before transitioning into acting. She has starred for several US and Canadian companies. Her successful modeling career has seen her feature in advertisement campaigns for Axe Deodrant, Chevrolet, Acura, Dentyne Ice, and Subway.

The actress made her TV debut with the teen comedy series Really Me (2012). It aired for 2 seasons and 26 episodes on Family Channel from 22nd April 2011 to 5th April 2013. Her other small-screen credits include The Flash (2014), Ransom (2017) and Beyond (2017-18).

In 2019, Lai featured as Aline Penhallow in the third season of the Supernatural drama series Shadowhunters. She made appearances in 4 episodes of the series.

As for the big screens, Lai made her movie debut in 2012 with the supernatural horror film Silent Hill: Revelation 3D directed by M.J. Bassett. It was a sequel to the psychological horror, Silent Hill (2006). In addition, she’s featured in short films Survivor Man (2013) and Asian Action (2014).

Meanwhile, the actress' upcoming movie Fall Back Down is currently in post-production. She's set to play the role of Lizzie.
