Triple H Offers To Help Pay Joel Embiids Fines So He Can Keep Using The DX Crotch Chop

June 2024 · 2 minute read

hopes to see more DX crotch chops from Philadelphia 76ers star Joel Embiid on the NBA court in the near future.

Earlier this week, D-Generation X was featured on the RAW is XXX special. This clearly made NBA superstar and longtime WWE fan Joel Emiid nostalgic for his childhood and prompted him to deliver a DX crotch chop celebration in a game against the Brooklyn Nets on January 25.

It has since come out that the NBA has fined Embiid $25,000 for doing this celebration, but Triple H is offering to help pitch in on his fine if he’ll continue to do it for the rest of the season. Tweeting out:

“Curious, @JoelEmbiid… If you wrote a bigger check now, could you get a volume discount to cover the rest of the season?? #IllChipIn #SuckIt,” Triple H posted.

Joel Embiid has yet to respond to The Game as of this writing, but earlier this week, Emiid responded to criticism from Kevin Durant, who called his DX crotch chop a “trash celebration” by telling him to suck it with a redone D-X video with him being placed into it.
