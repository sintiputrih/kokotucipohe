Richard Branson Net Worth: How Rich Is Richard Branson?

May 2024 · 1 minute read

Sir Richard Charles Nicholas Branson is a British billionaire, entrepreneur, and business magnate famous for founding The Virgin Group in the 1970s has an estimated net worth of $3.7 billion.

Branson’s Virgin brand grew very fast during the 1980s, as he started Virgin Atlantic airline and expanded the Virgin Records music label.

He formed Virgin Atlantic and Virgin Cargo in 1984. He formed Virgin Holidays a year later, in 1985.

In 1997, Branson founded the Virgin Rail Group to bid for passenger rail franchises during the privatisation of British Rail.

The Virgin Trains brand operated:

  • The InterCity West Coast franchise from 1997 to 2019,
  • The InterCity CrossCountry franchise from 1997 to 2007, and
  • The InterCity East Coast franchise from 2015 to 2018.
