Nigerian actress Seilat Adebowale has berated her colleague actress Chizzy Alichi over her insensitive comments following the crash of Funke Akindele’s marriage.

A lot of social media users; both concerned and unconcerned fans have shared their two cents about the failed marriage of actress Funke Akindele and her husband JJC Skillz.

After the husband of Funke Akindele took to his Instagram page to announce the end of their marriage, there was a lot of hullabaloo on social media. Fans expressed their disbelief at the piece of news shared by JJC Skillz.

A few minutes after JJC Skillz announced the end of his marriage with Funke Akindele, actress Chizzy Alichi took to her Instagram story to write that her marriage is working and will never crash. It seems a lot of people took offense to her post as many say that it was the wrong timing.

Colleague actress Seilat Adebowale has berated Chizzy over her comment. Seilat took to her Instagram story to berate Chizzy asking her why she won’t rather pray in her house or their mind rather than post it on her page at a serious point in the lives of others.

“Sad how people laugh over someone else’s predicament. And awon tean “my own marriage will work,”part of her post read.
