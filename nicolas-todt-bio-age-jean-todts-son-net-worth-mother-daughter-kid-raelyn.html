Nicolas Todt Biography

Todt also grew his name as a professional racer by moving from Sauber to Ferrari.

Todt was born and raised in Le Chesnay, Yvelines a suburb located in France. He was first home-schooled at the age of 10. Eventually, Todt finished his middle school studies in his hometown. He then graduated from a private high school in Yvelines and proceeded to the University. Nicolas then earned his Master’s degree in Management from Toulouse Business School in France.

Todt began his career as a motorsport racer through the influence of his father. Eventually, he was able to create and launch his own Management agency for French and global racing drivers called All Roads Management.
