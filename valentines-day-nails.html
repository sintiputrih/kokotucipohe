20 Valentine's Day nail looks we've fallen head over heels for

    Irrespective of your relationship status, Valentine's Day presents the ideal opportunity to up your mani game and indulge in some serious self-love.

    Forget showers of roses or elaborate grand gestures, this year we're all about perusing the plethora of stylish love-themed nail art looks out there, and choosing 'the one' to suit our February 14th vibe.

    Curating a feed full of super skilled nail techs is our forte – and this year we are utterly spoilt for choice.

    From glitzy heart gems to romantic French tips, the looks we've handpicked would not only be perfect for honouring Saint Valentine, lots of them could actually work all year round. Enter: your new nail crush...

    Hello! Fashion shares the Valentine's Day nail looks that we've fallen head over heels for:

    Aura Nails

    Hazy and vibey, aura nails tend feature a blurred circular centre, and this pink colourway feels utterly apt for Valentine's.

    Vibrant French Tips

    Slim French tips are making a return, and we love this colourful take on the mani classic.

    MORE: Winter nail colours that ooze cosy elegance

    READ: Bow Nails: 10 Coquettecore ideas to inspire your next manicure

    Pillar Box Red

    Not into elaborate nail art? No problem. Go for a solid colour classic with pillar box red. 

    Minimalist Red Hearts

    If you're wanting a subtle nod to the season, try red hearts layered over a blush pink squoval base.

    Glitter Tips With White Heart Accent

    Champion understated glitz with pink glitter tips and a single heart in a contrasting shade.

    Broken Heart 

    Expert manicurist Betina Goldstein's quirky, unforgettable nail art lives in my mind rent-free all year round, not just around Valentine's, and this heart-centric thumb look is just adorable.

    Gold Hearts

    Unswayed by the traditional red-pink colour scheme? Lean into metallics with molten gold hearts.

    Mix 'N' Match Moment 

    For a super playful Valentine's nail moment, opt for the mix 'n' match approach and try a look with different designs on each individual nail.

    Heart-Lined Tips 

    Tips needn't be uniform, and I love the fluidity of the pink line in this understated, heart-inspired mani look.

    Contrasting Coat Of Arms

    This graphic heart nail look reminds me of a modern, uber-romantic coat of arms - and I'm here for it.

    Holographic Gems

    If you're looking to dial up the glitz factor, consider layering heart gems over a wash of glitter. 

    Bold Tip Contrasts

    For a simple, high-impact look, alternate pink and red tips with heart detailing.

    Pink Chrome

    Cool-girl chromes are everywhere right now, and for Valentine's, a glossy raspberry finish is the way to go.

    French Tips And Pink Hearts 

    Embrace negative space by pairing square French tips alongside classic pink hearts. 

    Multi-Coloured Hearts

    Hearts are synonymous with Valentine's Day, and this look featuring lilac, white and punchy fuchsia feels super cute. 

    Coquette Cool-Girl 

    Bows are having a serious moment across both the fashion and beauty spheres. Hop on the trend and style it alongside sultry red tips for the season of love.

    Lovestruck Minimalism

    Forget wearing your heart on your sleeve, it's all about the nails. This manicure design features tiny, centrally-placed red hearts with gold lines down the centre, richly reminiscent of Cupid's golden arrow.

    Itsy Bitsy Hearts 

    Wanting to pay a super subtle tribute to Saint Valentine? Tiny cuticle-bordering hearts over pale pink is the look I'm currently crushing on.

    Almond French Tips 

    Amp up an otherwise understated almond French tip look with white hearts and sparkly gems.

    Red-Pink Check

    If you're into graphic impact, this red and pink checked design featuring a focal point heart is a striking choice.
