Liv Morgan Is Prepared To Roll In Thumbtacks And Get Set On Fire To Keep Her Title At Extreme Rules

is ready to go the extreme, even if it means literally going through fire.

Speaking with Rick Ucchino for Cageside Seats, Liv Morgan previewed her Extreme Rules match against Ronda Rousey on Saturday. Morgan says she’s prepared to do anything it takes to keep her title because she wants to prove how much she loves the pro wrestling business.

“There’s nothing that I won’t do. And I mean that so very genuinely. There’s absolutely nothing that I wouldn’t do. And there’s especially nothing that I wouldn’t do to keep this championship,” Morgan proclaimed. “Ronda’s gonna have to kill me. Like, I’ve said it before, but I just mean that so quite literally. You’re gonna have to get creative if you think anything’s gonna keep me down from keeping my championship.”

Morgan says she’s not sure how else to prove her love for wrestling besides putting her body on the line, even if it means going to extreme measures in a match.

“There’s a passion about it,” Morgan said. “You have to love something with your whole entire heart and soul. And I don’t know how else to show my love for wrestling than to like show you guys that I will do anything for this. I will put my body through anything just so you could understand how much this means to me and how much I love this. I don’t know how else to really explain it more than that. It’s like, for you to understand what this means to me, I will roll through thumbtacks, on fire.”
