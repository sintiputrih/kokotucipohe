Tyron Woodley Net Worth 2024

Tyron Woodley has an estimated net worth of $4 million. He earned most of his money fighting in the UFC. Additionally, he had sponsorship deals with brands like  Dobb Tires and Metabolic Meals. He took to boxing to make a living and fought Jake Paul twice. Unfortunately, he was on the receiving end of a loss on both occasions. Yet, it was not all in vain as he was estimated to earn well over $4 million for these 2 fights. That is almost as much as his career earnings from the UFC, which stands at around $4,874,425.

Tyron Woodley Professional MMA Career

Tyron Woodley made his MMA debut in Strikeforce in 2009. After an impressive record of 8 wins and one loss in Strikeforce, he was signed by the UFC. His UFC career started a bit rough, but he soon found his footing and became the welterweight champion in 2016. Woodley defeated “Ruthless” Robby Lawler to do so. The champion defended his title 4 times before losing it to Kamaru Usman in 2019.

After departing from the UFC he fought and lost to Jake Paul in boxing on two occasions. The knockout in their second fight hampered his reputation, and he has not fought professionally since.
