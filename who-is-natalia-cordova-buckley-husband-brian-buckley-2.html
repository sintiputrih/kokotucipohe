Natalia Cordova-Buckley Net Worth – How Rich Is She?

The estimated net worth of Natalia Cordova-Buckley is around $1 – $5 million as of 2022. The Mexican-American actress must have collected a decent sum from her career.

She seems to be enjoying her modest life based on her Instagram. Natalia made her acting debut at 26 in the Mexican comedy-drama series Los simuladores, 2008–2009. She got the role during the series’ fourth season of the 6-episode miniseries, Slingshot, released on December 13, 2016.

In the A&E horror drama series Bates Motel (2017), she had guest-starred as attorney Julia Ramos.

The same year, in the Disney-Pixar animated musical film Coco, she voiced the role of artist Frida Kahlo, which won the Academy Award for Best Animated Feature.

She had portrayed Det. Gavras in the crime thriller movie Destroyer, opposite to Nicole Kidman in 2018.
