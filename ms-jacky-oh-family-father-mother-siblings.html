Get To Know Ms Jacky Oh Family: Father, Mother, Siblings!

February 2024 · 4 minute read

TMZ has learned that Ms. Jacky Oh, a former “Wild ‘N Out” star and DC Young Fly’s longtime partner, has died at the age of 32. She reportedly died in Miami but the cause of her death is not public yet.

The MTV Wild ‘N’ Out star, whose real name is Jacklyn Smith, was reportedly receiving “mommy makeover” surgery in Miami with Dr. Zachary Okhah, according to rumors. The doctor has since deleted the picture off his page. After giving birth to her only son just a few months prior, Ms Jacky Oh went to the doctor for a mommy makeover on June 1, 2023, but allegedly passed away during the process.

The mother-of-three leaves behind her spouse and DC Young Fly as well as her three children, Nova, Nala, and Prince.

According to TMZ, the TV star, who was born John Whitfield, was learning that the mother of his children had passed away while he was recording new episodes of the sketch comedy series.

Here are a few details about Jacky Oh’s family members. This piece contains information about her father, mother, and siblings.

Ms Jacky Oh Family

Mc Jacky Oh rarely shares stories of her family on her socials.

One of the stories that included her family was in July 2016, when she wrote: “My dad makes my little brother run about 5 miles at 4 am every morning – & my dad follows behind him in his car. I asked my dad if he follows him because he thinks my brothers not gonna do it, or not run the full distance. & he said no- I drive behind him because I don’t want a police officer to shoot him in the back while he’s running just because he’s getting his exercise.”

In September 2018, Jacky took to her IG and wrote that her grandma has passed away. She wrote, “My strong, resilient, silly, outspoken, firecracker, true definition of a QUEEN! The matriarch of our family passed away tonight, I’m torn up inside but also know that she’s lived a long beautiful life with grandkids and great-grandkids. Thank you, God for giving us TIME to say our goodbyes, Rest In Peace Grampsie. I’ve got another angel looking over me.”

Who Is Ms Jacky Oh Father?

Ms Jacky Oh’s father is reportedly named Keith Smith. On her dad’s birthday on August 2018, Ms Jacky Oh wished: “To the best dad in the entire world, taught me all I know! Y’all tell my dad Happy Happy Birthday!”

On Father’s Day 2019, she posted this picture of her dad and captioned it: “Happy Daddy Day to all you amazing daddies in the world ❤️ Our Kings & Protectors.”

Comments on a few of her posts have revealed that Keith has a job in a bank. But, because he is not available on social media, not much detail could be unearthed.

Who Is Ms Jacky Oh Mother?

The only thing that we know of Ms Jacky Oh’s mother is that she is no longer married to Keith. Furthermore, her dad Keith has a new wife named Maria Smith, Jack Oh’s stepmother. Maria celebrates her birthday on 27 December.
