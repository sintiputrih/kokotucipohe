Richards’ energetic performances and charismatic personality quickly made him a fan favorite, and he became a prominent member of the social media collective known as the Sway House.

Richards’ rise to fame has also opened doors in the business world. He co-founded Ani Energy, an energy drink venture, and CrossCheck Studios, a production company.
