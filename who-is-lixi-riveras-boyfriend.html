Who Is Lixi Riveras Boyfriend?

May 2024 · 3 minute read

Lexi Rivera is in a relationship as of 2023, however, she previously dated YouTuber Ben Azelart between 2018 and 2020, and she is now dating Andrew Davila.

Alexa Brooke Rivera (born June 7, 2001) is a gymnast and internet personality from the United States. She rose to notoriety by appearing in her older brother Brent Rivera’s videos, and she then moved out to be her own content maker. She is presently signed to Amp Studios, which was founded by her brother Brent Rivera.

Ben Azelart: Lixi Rivera’s Ex- Boyfriend

Lexi allegedly met Ben Azelart (born January 10, 2002) in 2017, around the time Amp Studios was founded and creators were signing contracts with the company. Ben stated that they did not speak to each other for the first six months after the meeting. But they realized they liked each other and began dating in 2018. Between 2018 and 2020, the two frequently appeared in each other’s social media posts and in Brent Rivera’s videos.

Lexi announced their separation in a YouTube video titled “We Broke Up” in November 2020. They said that it was becoming difficult for them to pretend that everything was well on camera, but they also stated that the choice to split up was mutual, and the reason was not as dramatic as many may imagine.

They then documented the stages of their relationship, claiming that while they claimed to be happy and cheery on video, they were quite different when the camera was turned off. It got to the point where they couldn’t keep up the act any longer.

They also stated that they were quite young when the relationship began and that they have developed beyond it, claiming that they are better off as friends. They also mentioned that they squandered their opportunity to spend their early adulthood as self-sufficient individuals.

They ended the video by saying that they made a promise while they were together, that they would be friends even after they split up, and that they were open to dating one other in the future if the stars aligned. Here’s the video in which they announced their split.
