Kim Kardashian Is Obsessed with Kourtney Kardashian Oscars Look

May 2024 · 2 minute read

Fans cannot get over Kourtney Kardashian and Travis Barker's steamy PDA on the Oscars red carpet—and it seems neither can Kim Kardashian.

The SKIMS founder shared photos of her elder sister and her rocker fiancé at the 94th Academy Awards on her Instagram Story last night.

"This is so iconic," Kim wrote, along with a string of crying emojis, over a photo of Kourtney and Travis in their matching all-black looks on the Oscars carpet.

Momodu Mansaray//Getty Images

The Poosh founder wore a strapless black Thierry Mugler dress, Aquazzura sandals, Lorraine Schwartz jewelry, a clutch bag, and slicked-back hair, while Travis—who performed at the ceremony—looked dapper in a black suit and sunglasses.

"@travisbarker on the drums #Oscars," Kim wrote over a shot of the Blink-182 drummer performing at the ceremony.

While posing together on the carpet, the pair couldn't contain their love and did their signature kiss in front of the cameras.

Kevin Mazur//Getty Images

Kim was not present at the ceremony, though she and supermodel sister Kendall Jenner later joined Kourtney and Travis—a.k.a. Kravis—at the Vanity Fair Oscar party. Kim and Kendall coordinated in Balenciaga looks for the fete. Kim—the new face of the brand—wore a bright blue dress with built-in gloves, while Kendall opted for a dramatic black gown with ruffle detailing and a flowing train.

For the post-awards show bash, Kourtney changed into a vintage Dolce & Gabbana black-and-cream dress with black lace, silver details, and a rose appliqué.
