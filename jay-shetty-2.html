Jay Shetty is an English author, former hindu monk, journalist, entrepreneur, internet personality and life coach of British Indian descent. As of 2022, Jay Shetty’s net worth is $4 million. He is well known for writing books such as Life Changing Quotes (2022) and Think Like a Monk: Train Your Mind for Peace and Purpose Every Day (2020).

He is the recipient of the Streamy Award for Best Podcast (2020) and Streamy Award for Health and Wellness (2018).

Jay Shetty Wiki/Biography

Born on 6 September 1987, Jay Shetty’s age is 35 Years Old as of 2022. He was born in a well-settled family from Barnet, United Kingdom. He holds a British nationality and has his belief in Hinduism religion. His zodiac sign is Virgo.
