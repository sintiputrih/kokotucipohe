Kızılcık Şerbeti, also known as The Cranberry Sherbet, is a Turkish television series that first aired in January 2021. The show follows the story of Nursema, a young woman who returns to her hometown to start a new life after her husband's death. She becomes involved with Umut, a man who owns a local cranberry juice factory, and they work together to overcome various challenges and obstacles.

The series features a talented cast, including Barış Kılıç, Evrim Alasya, Sıla Türkoğlu, Sibel Taşçıoğlu, Müjde Uzman, Doğukan Güngör and Settar Tanrıöğen.
