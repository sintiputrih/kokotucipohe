Anderson Cooper | I-Biography

Kwabaningi, umqondo wokuba usozigidi ngokubhala nokukhuluma ngabantu ubonakala ungenakufezeka, kodwa lobu ubuqiniso bentatheli edumile yaseMelika. Anderson Cooper owaqongelela izigidi zamaRandi ngokwenza ukuhlaziya okufanelekile kwezepolitiki nezinye izindaba ze-vial ku-TV. 

Ungumkhokeli oyinhloko wohlelo lwezindaba lwe-CNN u-Anderson Cooper 360 degrees kanye nentatheli imizuzu engu-60. Umshicileli wezindaba womhlaba wonke waqala umsebenzi wakhe wokuba imodeli kodwa wagxumela phezulu kakhulu ebuntathelini ngobuchule nobungcweti bakhe. Manje, usengomunye wobuso obaziwa kakhulu ezindabeni ze-CNN.

U-Anderson Cooper Partner, ubani umyeni wakhe?

U-Anderson Cooper gay, kanye nenye yezintatheli ezidume kakhulu zezitabane ku-American TV ngemuva kokuthi ekugcineni evule isifuba ngempilo yakhe yangasese ngo-2012 phambi kwabalandeli bakhe.

Ekhuluma ngesimo sakhe sobudlelwane, ngaphambili, wayesebudlelwaneni noCesar Recio. Baqala ukuthandana ngonyaka ka-2004, kwathi ngemuva kokuphola iminyaka eminingana, bahlukana ngo-2009.

Wabe eseqala ukuthandana nomnikazi wendawo yokudlela oyisitabane uBenjamin Antoine Maisani. Baqale ngoFebhuwari 2009. Baneminyaka eminingi bethandana, futhi manje bayashada maduze.

I-Anderson Cooper inenani elifanele

Izindaba ezidumile ukuthi uthola isamba esingu-$12 million njalo ngonyaka CNN, esenyuse inani lakhe lafinyelela ku-$210 million kusukela ngo-2020.

Ukuphakama Nesisindo

Ngokwezilinganiso zomzimba wakhe, unobude obungamamitha angu-5 nama-intshi angu-10, futhi unesisindo esingama-68 kg.
